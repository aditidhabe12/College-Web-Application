Training & Placements

Campus placements play a major role in shaping up the career goals of students. It is the dream of every engineering student to get placed in a top organization visiting their campus for recruitment. Recruiters visiting engineering colleges seek for candidates who are ready for the industry and have the etiquettes necessary for the corporate world.


During campus placements, recruiters test for an array of skill sets in their potential employees. In addition to being knowledgeable in their core subjects, students should also possess great aptitude, reasoning and soft skills. We at KBJIIT regularly organize training programs to sharpen the skills of students.

    List of Training Programs

  • Communication Skills Training to FE Students.
  • Aptitude Training to Third Year Students.
  • Mock Aptitude Tests (Written and Online) For Final Year Students.
  • Interview Preparation Session for Final Year Students by TCS.
  • Mock GD / PI Sessions for Final Year Students.
  • Mock Aptitude Tests (Written and Online) For Final Year Students.
  • Language Lab for Improving Language Skills.
  • Aptitude Lab for Improving Aptitude Skills.
