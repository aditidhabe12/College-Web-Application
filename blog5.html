Counselling Session for Students

  • 6 Feb,

The Counseling Session for Students is a supportive and confidential program designed to provide students with a safe space to discuss their academic, personal, and emotional concerns. Led by qualified counselors or mental health professionals, the session aims to offer guidance, support, and resources to help students navigate challenges and improve their overall well-being.


The counseling session may be offered as a one-time event or as a recurring program.

The Counselling Session Team : Mr. Kulkarni and Ms. Joshi
Date & time for session : Every Tuesday, 12:00 PM



The counseling session is open to all students enrolled in the college or university. It is suitable for students experiencing academic, personal, or emotional challenges and seeking support, guidance, or resources.

Registration for the counseling session may be required to ensure adequate space and resources for participants. Students can register through team or by contacting the counseling center directly.

The Counseling Session for Students is organized by the college or university's counseling center, in collaboration with mental health professionals, faculty members, and student support services. The organizers are committed to promoting student well-being and providing accessible and inclusive mental health support to all students.



This Counseling Session for Students aims to create a supportive and empowering environment where students can openly discuss their concerns, learn coping strategies, and access resources to enhance their mental health and well-being during their college journey.
